2. Market & Finance Context

Argentina's renewable energy finance model is evolving with direct implications for bankability.

Current Model:

Shift from centralized, sovereign-wrapped PPAs (RenovAr) to a mixed regime of private corporate PPAs (MATER), targeted tenders, and wholesale market (MEM) liberalization.

Key Programs:

  • RenovAr: Legacy program with USD PPAs & FODER/World Bank guarantees
  • MATER: Bilateral corporate PPAs; ~10 GW awarded 2018-2025
  • AlmaGBA: 2025 BESS tender with 15-year USD contracts, backstopped by CAMMESA
  • RenMDI: Tender to replace forced generation
Primary Risk: FX Convertibility - Historic controls and payment chain arrears are key deterrents. The RIGI law and ongoing capital control reforms are pivotal for attracting foreign investment.
